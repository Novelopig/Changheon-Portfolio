World famous chocolate events

Salon du Chocolat

A globally renowned chocolate festival held annually in Paris, France. It features a variety of chocolate products, along with chocolate fashion shows and design exhibitions.

Eurochocolate

The largest chocolate festival in Europe, held annually in Perugia, Italy, in October. Chocolate producers from various European countries gather to showcase a wide range of chocolates.

Chocolate Festival of Belize

Held annually in May in Belize, this festival offers visits to cocoa farms, chocolate-making workshops, and tours of cocoa and chocolate hotspots.
